WHY PROJECT KAYAMANAN?

PK is a fun, creative, and reward based personal finance program. It is also the first online peer financial education program for high school students.

About Us

Project Kayamanan is an online peer financial coaching sessions to public senior high school students in the Philippines as well as individual coaching sessions for students with families, who are needing personal financial advices. Project Kayamanan seeks to impart fun, simple, creative and interesting financial education program that high school students can easily teach to other students or peers.

Our Objectives

1

Introduce basic financial concepts

2

Teach how to access financial services

3

Encourage building savings & assets

4

Support mindset in overcoming vulnerability

5

Urge planning for economic security

6

Share the value of reciprocity and giving back

7

Promote equality, fairness & justice
