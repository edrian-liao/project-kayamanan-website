Our Programs

Community Programs

PK

PK is a free online peer financial education program for the Filipino youth ages 15 to 24 years old. It follows train-the-trainer, peer education and rewards-based model. It is initially funded by YSEALI Seeds for the Future and supported by US State Department and US Mission to ASEAN.

PK WORKSHOPS

It is a free monthly online trainings/seminars offered to Project Kayamanan members by guest mentors. It will boost the members' confidence and provide them with new skills and abilities, which will help them become economically independent, passionate and purposeful

OPLAN KAALAMAN SA PANANALAPI

It is a free financial education program for low-income rural communities. It trains influential young people--usually youth leaders--in the community to be an OKSP peer educator. OKSP peer educators facilitate financial education sessions to their community and will provide one-on-one coaching to families, especially mothers, who are the primary person concerned in cash handling in the family.
