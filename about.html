What is Project Kayamanan?

Project Kayamanan is an online peer financial coaching sessions to public senior high school students in the Philippines as well as individual coaching sessions for students with families, who are needing personal financial advices. Project Kayamanan seeks to impart fun, simple, creative and interesting financial education program that high school students can easily teach to other students or peers.
